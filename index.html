Conventional NDT Solutions

Time-tested inspection methods delivering reliable defect detection for routine maintenance, fabrication quality control, and in-service verification. Our certified inspectors utilize industry-standard techniques to ensure code compliance and asset safety.

Visual Testing (VT)

Direct visual examination of surfaces, welds, and components for surface-breaking discontinuities.

Ultrasonic Testing (UT)

Thickness measurement and flaw detection using high-frequency sound waves for internal defects.

Magnetic Particle (MT)

Surface and near-surface defect detection in ferromagnetic materials using magnetic fields.

Additional Methods: Liquid Penetrant (PT), Radiographic Testing (RT), Eddy Current (ET), Leak Testing (LT), IR Thermography, PMI, and Hardness Testing.

Tubing Systems

Heat Exchanger & Tube Inspection

Specialized non-destructive testing for heat exchangers, boilers, air coolers, and condensers. We detect corrosion, pitting, cracks, and wall loss in tubing systems to maintain thermal efficiency and prevent leaks.

Eddy Current (ECT)

High-speed detection of pits, cracks, and wall thinning in non-ferromagnetic tubes. Ideal for heat exchangers and condensers.

Remote Field (RFT)

Electromagnetic inspection for ferromagnetic tubes (carbon steel) detecting internal and external defects with equal sensitivity.

IRIS

Internal Rotating Inspection System using ultrasonic immersion for precise wall thickness measurements and accurate defect sizing.

Additional Methods: Near Field Testing (NFT) for air cooler fins, Magnetic Flux Leakage (MFL) for ferrous tubes, and specialized probes for U-bends.

AI Integration in Asset Integrity Management

SIT provides AI-enabled workflows that enhance inspection planning, defect detection, data quality, and risk-based decision making. We convert inspection data into actionable intelligence inside your AIM platform.

Automated Defect Screening

Computer vision models flag corrosion, coating breakdown, deformation, and anomalies in drone imagery for engineer review.

Assisted Reporting

AI structures findings, standardizes reporting language, and reduces reporting time with engineer validation.

Data Extraction from Legacy Docs

OCR/NLP extraction of inspection history from PDFs/scans into structured databases for AIM/RBI use.

Predictive Analytics

Trend analysis for thickness loss, remaining life forecasting, and early-warning indicators on critical circuits.

Human-in-the-loop workflow: AI suggests and flags; qualified personnel review, confirm, and finalize findings. AI outputs are always validated by certified inspection personnel.

Typical Scope of Work

Pre-Job Review

Review of coating system specification, data sheets, approved procedures, ITPs, and hold points.

Surface Preparation

Final Acceptance

Holiday (pinhole) detection, adhesion testing where specified, visual acceptance, and completion records.
